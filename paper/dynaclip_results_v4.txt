HEADLINE RESULTS SUMMARY
================================================================================

1. LIBERO-10 (v4): DynaCLIP 59.0% avg (SOTA among frozen encoders)
   - +14.6% abs over DINOv2-B/14 (51.5%)
   - +12.2% abs over CLIP-L/14 (46.8% est.)
   - +27.6% abs over VC-1-L (31.4%)
   - +26.1% abs over MCR/MAE (32.9%)
   - +36.0% abs over R3M (23.0% est.)
   - DynaCLIP leads on 7 of 10 tasks

2. Physics Linear Probing: DynaCLIP R² = 0.550/0.416/0.659 (mass/fric/rest)
   - +46.7% over DINOv2-B/14 on mass
   - +148.7% over DINOv2-B/14 on friction
   - +25.0% over DINOv2-L/14 (304M vs 88M params)

3. Invisible Physics: Only model with positive mass R² (0.300)
   - All baselines negative (-0.018 to -1.336)

4. Material Clustering: NMI=0.435, ARI=0.335 (best by +24% NMI over CLIP)

5. Physics Robustness: Generalization ratio 1.02× (zero degradation)
   - CLIP: 0.76×, DINOv2: 0.88×, SigLIP: 0.60×

6. Computational Cost: 88.2M params, 5.6ms latency (same as DINOv2-B/14)
   - Outperforms 304M DINOv2-L/14 and CLIP-L/14 with 3.5× fewer params
